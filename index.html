Foundational Protocols

Universal DID-Native Addressing (UDNA)

W3C COMMUNITY GROUP

A project to redefine internet addressing by making Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs) a first-class primitive in network protocols. It aims to solve the internet's "original sin" by tying communication to verified identity, not just location. This work is actively developed within a dedicated W3C Community Group to ensure it becomes a global standard.

Codon Protocol

RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT

A programmable intent and coordination layer designed for the post-AI Internet. It enables new forms of trust, identity, and collaboration between humans, organizations, and autonomous AI agents.
