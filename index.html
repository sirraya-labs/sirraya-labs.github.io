Sirraya Labs

Research-driven systems lab building real-time health and emergency infrastructure

Sirraya Labs focuses on designing and validating mission-critical systems where latency, correctness, and reliability are non-negotiable. Our work spans emergency response infrastructure, real-time distributed systems, and medically constrained decision logic.

Focus Areas

  • Adaptive emergency and disaster-response systems
  • Real-time health logistics and coordination
  • Distributed, fault-tolerant infrastructure
  • Safety-first and verifiable system design

Flagship Work

BloodNet — An adaptive emergency blood logistics platform designed for real-world crisis response, donor safety, and medical correctness.

Open & Responsible Engineering

We believe critical infrastructure benefits from transparency, peer review, and simulation-based validation. Where possible, we open interfaces, protocols, and research artifacts while maintaining clear boundaries for regulated systems.

Collaboration

We work with hospitals, blood banks, public-sector agencies, and research partners. If you are interested in pilots, research collaboration, or systems review, please reach out.
